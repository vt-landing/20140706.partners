Project VT

A community based bible studies platform.

Soon to be launched, Project VT is a learning platform to help the religious intellectual take rich bible studies courses online. The goal of this project is to better connect people with their form of faith, and be a beacon of learning, tolerance, and community. This project intents to partner with the religious institutions arround the world, and adapt their bible study programs into massively open online courses.
